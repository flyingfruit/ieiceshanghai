Welcome to IEICE Malaysia

Establishment of IEICE Malaysia Section

It is our great pleasure to introduce the establishment of the IEICE Malaysia Section! The IEICE Overseas Section has been evolved since it was commenced with four regional Representatives in 2003, and the number of Sections was extended to eleven Sections in 2012. Further, it was reformed to the current IEICE International Section program in 2014 in order to promote autonomous regional academic activities in a multicultural society. IEICE Malaysia Section was officially established as the twelfth IEICE International Section with the authorization by the IEICE Board in February 2015.

An extensive member recruitment drive will be conducted throughout the month of December-2019, stay tuned.
